Our story

Your Trusted Mobile Mechanic in Dallas

To provide convenient, high-quality auto repair wherever you are, with transparent pricing and exceptional customer service.

Mobile Mechanic 24/7 was founded in Dallas with a simple mission: make car repair as easy as possible. We saw that traditional shops required towing, long waits, and often unclear pricing. So we built a mobile service that brings the mechanic to you – saving time, money, and hassle.

Over the past 12 years, we've grown from a single van to a fleet of fully equipped service vehicles. Our team of ASE-certified mechanics shares a passion for cars and a commitment to honest work. We've helped thousands of Dallas drivers get back on the road quickly and safely, earning a reputation for reliability and fair dealing.
