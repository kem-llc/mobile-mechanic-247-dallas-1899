Our story

Your Mobile Mechanic in Dallas

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair without the hassle of a shop visit. We believe in transparency, convenience, and getting you back on the road safely.

Mobile Mechanic 24/7 was founded by a team of ASE-certified technicians who saw a need for convenient, trustworthy auto repair in Dallas. Tired of long waits at shops and hidden fees, we built a mobile service that brings the shop to you. Our vans are fully equipped to handle most repairs on the spot, from diagnostics to major component replacements.

Today, we serve hundreds of Dallas drivers each year, earning a reputation for reliability and fair pricing. Every mechanic shares our commitment to quality and customer education. We don't just fix cars — we build relationships based on trust.
