Our story

Dallas’s Mobile Mechanic – Founded on Trust and Convenience

To provide Dallas drivers with honest, high-quality auto repairs wherever they are – no towing, no hassle, no hidden costs.

Mobile Mechanic 24/7 was born from a simple frustration: waiting days for a repair shop appointment and paying for a tow. Our founder, a veteran ASE-certified mechanic, saw an opportunity to bring the shop to the customer. Starting with a single van, we’ve grown into a fleet serving the entire Dallas area, earning a reputation for reliability and transparency.

Today, our team of certified technicians handles everything from minor maintenance to major engine work – all at your home, office, or roadside. We believe in fair pricing, clear communication, and getting you back on the road as quickly as possible. Every repair is backed by our warranty and our promise to treat your car like it’s our own.
