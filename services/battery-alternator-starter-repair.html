Quick diagnosis and replacement for starting and charging issues.

Overview

Dead battery? Clicking starter? Dim lights? We'll test your battery, alternator, and starter system and replace any faulty component on-site.

What's included

  • Battery load testing
  • Alternator output check
  • Starter diagnosis and replacement

How it works

1

System Test

We use a digital analyzer to test battery health, alternator output, and starter draw.

2

Component Replacement

We replace the failing part with a new or remanufactured unit.

3

Re-test

We verify the charging system is working properly before we leave.
